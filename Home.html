Sara El-Metwally, Principal Investigator 

We are a research group in the Computer Science Department at Faculty of Computers and Information, Mansoura University, Egypt. As a Computational Biology Research group, our research deals with developing efficient computational tools for processing and analyzing biological data including DNA, RNA, and Protein sequences. Also, we are utilizing machine and deep learning techniques to study and gain insights of the genomic data secrets. As a Computer Science Research group, our research aims to integrate the technology to overcome the challenges that we are facing in the Egyptian society in different sectors related to Industry, Education, Agriculture and Health.
